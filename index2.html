ABOUT THE LOCATION

Zhovkva is located 25 kilometers from Lviv. Almost due north. You can get here by minibus from Lviv bus station No. 2 (buses run quite often, it takes half an hour). If you use your own transport (again from Lviv), then from Bohdan Khmelnytskyi Street on the northern outskirts of the city, you need to turn left. The road to Zhovkva passes not far from the picturesque Roztochchi ridge. Zhovkva also lies under it (ridge).

As we can see, there are enough architectural monuments and interesting places in Zhovkva. And when you are in Zhovkva and want to eat, you will find a variety of restaurants here. The kitchen here is great.
